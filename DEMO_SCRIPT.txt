[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's walk through the architecture. When a farmer submits a query, it starts at the START node and immediately goes through our Security Checkpoint node. This checkpoint scrubs any PII like Aadhaar numbers or phone numbers and filters prompt injections. If the input is approved, it reaches the orchestrator sub-agent. The orchestrator delegates tasks to our specialized LlmAgents: the agronomist agent for crop disease diagnosis and weather advisories, and the market analyst agent for mandi market rates. These sub-agents query our local Model Context Protocol, or MCP, server to fetch real-world data like prices and advisories. Finally, the response is processed by our verifier node. If it includes chemical pesticide suggestions or financial transactions, it triggers a Human-in-the-Loop review, pausing for expert agronomist approval before reaching the final output node. (pause)
